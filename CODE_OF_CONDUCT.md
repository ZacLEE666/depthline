# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and low-noise project community.

Be considerate, assume good intent, disagree with ideas rather than people, and protect private information. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, or repeated disruptive behavior are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers rather than escalating them in public issues.
